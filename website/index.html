A Complete End-to-End Solution

Vibe Venue isn't just an app; it's a fully autonomous local ecosystem. It solves the biggest problem at crowded stadiums, concerts, and festivals: terrible 4G/5G reception. Here is exactly what our platform does out-of-the-box:

Local Web Server

The vendor's Android device acts as an autonomous HTTP server. Attendees connect to the device's Wi-Fi hotspot and access a blazing-fast, dynamic HTML ordering page directly from their browser.

Voice-Activated Orders

In a loud stadium, looking at a screen isn't always possible. Our app uses advanced Text-to-Speech (TTS) to announce incoming orders, quantities, and customer names out loud to the kitchen staff.

Advanced Crowd Control

No more physical queues blocking the concourse! Orders are attached to specific "Zones" or "Seat Numbers", allowing dedicated runners to deliver food straight to the attendee without them ever leaving the event.

Deep Analytics & Export

Get real-time insights: track cash vs. online payments, monitor the best-selling items, and export all completed deliveries straight to a CSV file for end-of-day accounting.

Seamless Order Progression Flow

1. Attendee Submits (Pending)

The attendee scans the local QR code, builds a custom cart (with extras/add-ons), enters their seat location, and hits submit. The vendor's tablet rings instantly with a voice alert.

2. Kitchen Acknowledges (Preparing)

With one tap, the vendor marks the order as "Preparing". Using our Rush Mode setting, the app can even automatically auto-start preparation for massive order spikes.

3. Runner Hand-off (Ready & Delivered)

Once the food is plated, it's marked "Ready". A runner picks it up and delivers it to the attendee's seat. The vendor logs the final payment (Cash/UPI), capturing exact revenue and tips.

Quick Start: What to do after installing

  1. Setup Profile: Open the app and set your Stall/Zone Name in the settings so attendees know who they are ordering from.
  2. Add Menu Items: Go to the "Manage Menu" tab to add your items. You can create custom fields like dropdowns or checkboxes for addons.
  3. Enable Hotspot: Turn on your Android device's built-in Wi-Fi Hotspot (no internet data required).
  4. Start Server: Navigate to the "Ordering Mode" tab and tap "Start Server". A large QR code will appear on your screen.
  5. Receive Orders: Attendees simply connect to your hotspot, scan the QR code with their camera, and place orders directly from their browser!
