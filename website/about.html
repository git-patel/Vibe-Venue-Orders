About the App

Vibe Venue Orders is an innovative offline ordering solution designed to drastically improve the physical event experience for attendees at large-scale venues, whether it's a massive sporting event, a crowded music festival, or a bustling exhibition.

Large crowds mean congested cellular networks and massive queues at food stalls, leading to frustrated attendees missing the main event. We've built an offline-first technology to solve this exact problem: your device acts as a local server, attendees connect directly via Wi-Fi without needing internet, and they can order food straight to their location.

The Challenge

The goal is to "Design a solution that improves the physical event experience for attendees at large-scale venues, addressing crowd movement, waiting times, and real-time coordination."

Vibe Venue completely eliminates waiting times by turning every spot in the crowd into a point-of-sale. Crowd movement is minimized since people no longer rush the stalls all at once. Real-time coordination is handled locally on the vendor's dashboard.

FAQ

Do I need internet?
No. The app runs 100% offline. You use your phone’s Wi‑Fi hotspot; customers connect to it and open the menu in their browser.
Do customers need to install an app?
No. They only scan the QR code, join your hotspot, and order in the browser. No download required.
What does it cost?
No monthly fees. The app is free and open-source. You just need an Android phone.
Can I track payments?
Yes. When you mark an order delivered, you record cash or online payment. The app shows today’s payments and analytics (cash vs online, totals).
Does it work in Hindi?
Yes. You can enable Hindi in the app and use Hindi TTS for order announcements.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend/App: Flutter (Dart) with Material 3 UI.
  • Server: Dart shelf running a lightweight HTTP server locally.
  • Database: sqflite for completely local, relational data storage.
  • Attendee UI: Responsive, zero-dependency raw HTML/JS injected dynamically by the Flutter server.
  • Text-to-Speech: Native Android TTS integration for accessibility and hands-free operations.

Future Roadmap

  • Mesh Networking: Allow multiple stalls to link together over a single mesh network.
  • Local Payment Gateway: Implement local UPI bridging that allows payment processing to queue and sync once a master node finds a sliver of internet connection.
  • Runner Companion App: A secondary app for delivery runners that connects to the vendor's hotspot to receive live routing maps of the stadium seats.
