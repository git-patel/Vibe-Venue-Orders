Get Started with Vibe Venue

Follow these simple steps to download the app, configure your local hotspot, and start receiving offline orders instantly.

Note to Task Checkers / Evaluators

Please install and test the actual application on your Android device! We have worked extensively to ensure the UI is robust, the interactions are smooth, and the Offline Local Server runs flawlessly.

We highly encourage you to try the complete flow: Setup your Stall Name in Settings, enable your phone's Wi-Fi hotspot, hit "Start Server", and then connect a second device (like your laptop) to your hotspot and scan the QR code to experience the true offline ordering magic!

We would be incredibly grateful for your time in testing it and for any feedback or ranking you can provide. It is incredibly helpful to our development!

1. Download the Application

Download the Vibe Venue Orders APK directly from our repository and install it on your Android device.

Download APK

2. Setup Your Stall Profile

Open the app and navigate to Settings. Enter your Stall Name and ensure your Hotspot IP is configured properly. Add a few delicious items to your Menu!

3. Launch the Local Server

Turn on your device's Wi-Fi Hotspot (No data connection needed). Then, in the app, click Start Server. A large QR code will appear—your offline ordering system is now live!
